Red Thai Curry Soup

Add the curry paste to a pan on the hob and cook it over a high heat for 2 minutes until the paste has loosened slightly. Pour in the coconut milk and stir through for another minute to mix with the paste then remove from the heat. Roughly chop the chicken thighs and place in the slow cooker with all of the chopped vegetables. Sprinkle over the corn flour, salt and pepper to coat the vegetables and meat. BAsh the lemon grass stalk with a rolling pin to open up the flavour and add into the cooking pot. Pour over the coconut milk and curry paste sauce ensuring the meat and vegetables are completely covered. Add the sugar, lime juice and soy sauce into the sauce and stir through. Cook on high for 4 hours or 7 hours on low.
